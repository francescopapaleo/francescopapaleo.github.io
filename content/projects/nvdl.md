_"Soggettiva attorno al 14 dicembre 2010"_

Non Vengo dalla Luna is a documentary theatre piece born in the aftermath of the December 14, 2010 demonstrations in Rome. Faced with an official narrative that erased many lived experiences, we collected first-person accounts—intimate stories where private life and political struggle intertwine—and reworked them into a stage form that privileges voice, memory, and listening.

- Text and performance: Carla Vitantonio

- Music and sound design: Francesco Papaleo

- Dramaturgy: from testimonies originally published on Global Project

- Special thanks: the authors of the original texts, Global Project, c.s. Tpo, Radio Kairòs, Oh! The Lady Stone
